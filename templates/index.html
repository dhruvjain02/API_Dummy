Pre-Imaging

Document

Pre-imaging documentation is crucial in mobile forensics to ensure evidence integrity and legal admissibility. Investigators must record the device's make, model, serial number, IMEI, MAC address, power state, battery level, physical condition, and connected peripherals. Connectivity settings, acquisition details, and chain of custody must also be logged to maintain a proper audit trail.
